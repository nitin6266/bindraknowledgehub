Senior UX Designer,

Frontend Architect,

Your objective is to transform the current homepage into a premium,
high-converting experience.

Before making any changes:

Read

AGENTS.md

Review all existing homepage sections.

Do NOT create new pages.

Do NOT redesign the design system.

==============================================================================
MISSION
==============================================================================

The homepage currently contains too much content.

There is significant repetition because detailed pages now exist.

Your responsibility is to make the homepage lighter,
faster,
cleaner,
and more conversion-focused.

The homepage should convince parents to contact the academy.

It should NOT contain every piece of information.

Think of the homepage as an executive summary.
